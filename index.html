PuffTab

A comprehensive cannabis consumption tracking app for iOS.

Overview

PuffTab is a feature-rich iOS application designed to help users track and manage their cannabis consumption with precision and insight. Whether you're a medical patient monitoring dosage or a recreational user tracking expenses, PuffTab provides the tools you need to understand your consumption patterns.

Key Features

Comprehensive Tracking
  • Detailed Puff Logging: Record each session with timestamp, amount (grams), method, and supply used
  • Photo Documentation: Attach photos to your puffs for visual reference
  • Markdown Notes: Rich text notes with formatting support for detailed observations
  • Method Tracking: Track consumption by different methods (joint, pipe, bong, vaporizer, etc.)
Supply Management
  • Complete Inventory System: Track multiple supplies with strain details, THC/CBD content, and terpene profiles
  • Cost Tracking: Monitor cost per gram and total expenditure
  • Automated Depletion: Supplies automatically update as you log consumption
  • Supply Timeline: Visual history of finished supplies organized by month
  • Expiry Tracking: Set and monitor expiration dates for your supplies
Terpene Profiles
  • Detailed Terpene Tracking: Record individual terpenes and their percentages
  • Effect Information: View common effects and aromas for each terpene
  • Supply Associations: Link terpene profiles to specific supplies
Business Relationships
  • Shop Management: Track where you purchase your supplies
  • Brand Tracking: Organize supplies by brand
  • Licensed Producer Support: Link brands to their licensed producers
  • Rating System: Rate shops, brands, and supplies
Analytics & Insights
  • Dashboard Overview: At-a-glance view of today's consumption, costs, and trends
  • Daily Averages: Track average consumption and spending patterns
  • Supply Duration Analytics: Understand how long different supplies last
  • Usage Goals: Set and monitor daily, weekly, and monthly consumption goals
  • Visual Charts: Progress bars and statistics for inventory and consumption
Smart Features
  • Quick Access Methods: Pin frequently used consumption methods
  • Smart Search: Search across all supplies, brands, shops, and methods
  • Voice Commands: Siri shortcuts for hands-free logging
  • Customizable Time Display: Toggle between 12-hour and 24-hour time formats
User Experience
  • Intuitive Navigation: Tab-based interface for easy access to all features
  • Timeline Views: Browse puff history and supply history in chronological timelines
  • Swipe Actions: Quick delete functionality for puffs
  • Empty States: Helpful guidance when getting started
  • Dark Mode Support: Automatic adaptation to system appearance
Data Management
  • Import/Export: Full data backup and restore capabilities
  • Multiple Export Options:
    • Full export with photos
    • Text-only export for smaller file sizes
    • Photos-only export
  • Privacy Focused: All data stored locally on device
  • Batch Operations: Clear history and manage multiple items efficiently

Benefits

  • Precise Dosage Tracking: Monitor exact consumption amounts for medical compliance
  • Effect Documentation: Track effectiveness and side effects with detailed notes
  • Strain Analysis: Understand which strains work best for your conditions
  • Reporting Ready: Export data for healthcare provider consultations

  • Budget Management: Track spending and identify cost-saving opportunities
  • Consumption Awareness: Understand your usage patterns and make informed decisions
  • Variety Exploration: Keep track of different strains and their effects
  • Social Sharing: Document memorable experiences with photos and notes

  • Inventory Prevention: Never run out unexpectedly with low supply alerts
  • Quality Tracking: Rate and remember which products you prefer
  • Historical Insights: Learn from past consumption patterns

Getting Started

  1. Initial Setup: On first launch, the app seeds default consumption methods
  2. Add a Shop: Start by adding where you purchase supplies
  3. Add Brands: Create brand entries for your preferred products
  4. Add Supplies: Log your current inventory with details
  5. Start Tracking: Use Quick Puff or Add Puff to begin logging consumption

Privacy & Security

PuffTab is designed with privacy in mind:
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No internet connection required for core functionality
  • No user accounts or cloud sync
  • Complete control over your data with export/import features
